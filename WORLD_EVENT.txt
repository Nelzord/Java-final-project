Welcome to Srirag's and Jack's CalPoly Simulator:

1) How To Trigger The Event
Start by your mouse to click anywhere in the world to spawn a staging platform(A collection of Cal poly Logo's) to summon Jeffrey Armstrong.

2) What happens when the Event is triggered
The staging platform is permanently put in the world while the Event also affects the closest Blacksmith to become a OreBlob hungry 805.

What is the New Entity and what does it do
There are two new entities in this simulation:
	a. Jeffrey Armstrong: A satirical version of our president who goes around the world hungry for money.
	b. 805: A morphed Blacksmith going around and eating the oreblobs.
